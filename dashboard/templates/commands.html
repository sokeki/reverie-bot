📖 Commands

everything Reverie can do

Everyone

/points
Check your dream points, voice time, rank and streak. Add @user to check someone else.
/leaderboard
See the Hall of Dreamers. Sortable by points, rank, voice time or messages. Use top to change how many are shown.
/shop
Browse the dream shop. 5 items per page, sorted by cost.
/buy <item>
Purchase an item from the shop. Points are deducted instantly.
/inventory
See the items you own. Add @user to check someone else's inventory.
/settitle <title>
Equip a purchased title to display on your /points profile. Leave empty to remove your title.
/setcustomtitle <text>
Use a Custom Title item to set your own unique title (max 32 characters). Consumes the item on use. Buy another to change it.
/rolepreview <item>
Preview a role item's colour before buying. The embed sidebar shows the exact role colour.
/removerole
Use a Role Remover item to remove one of your purchased shop roles. Shows a dropdown of eligible roles.
/dashboard
Posts the dashboard link in chat with a preview image.

Valorant

/randomagent
Get a random Valorant agent. Use the role option to filter by Duelist, Initiator, Controller or Sentinel.
/randomrole
Get a random Valorant role.
/randomcomp
Randomly assign 5 members to Valorant roles. Enable roll_agents to also roll a random agent for each role.

Invite role only

/guestinvite
Generate a one-use guest invite that expires in 10 minutes. The invite link is sent to your DMs. When the guest joins the server and enters any voice channel, they are moved to your VC automatically. They are kicked when they leave.
/drag @member
Drag a member with the lingering role into your current voice channel. Both you and the target must be in a VC.

Admin only

/addpoints @user <amount>
Manually add or remove dream points from a member. Use a negative number to remove points.
/additem
Add an item to the dream shop. Choose from Role, Title, Custom Title or Role Remover. Can also be done from the dashboard.
/removeitem <name>
Remove an item from the shop by name. Can also be done from the dashboard.
/edititem <name>
Edit an existing shop item's name, cost, description or linked role. Can also be done from the dashboard.
/setinviterole @role
Set the role required to generate guest invites and use /drag.
/setlingeringrole @role
Set the role that members must have to be dragged with /drag.
