What is Reverie?

Reverie is a Discord bot built around a points/shop economy, competitive Valorant and TFT tracking, and a few community mini-games. Points are earned from chatting and voice activity, and spent in the dream shop on roles, titles, and comp-roll items. If you have a Riot account, register it to get automatic rank updates and stat lookups posted in your server.
