What is Reverie?

Reverie is a Discord bot built around a points/shop economy, competitive Valorant and TFT tracking, and a few community mini-games. Points are earned from chatting and voice activity, and spent in the dream shop on roles, titles, and comp-roll items. If you have a Riot account, register it to get automatic rank updates and stat lookups posted in your server.

Everyone

/dashboard
Get the link to this dashboard.

Points

/points [@user]
Check your dream points, voice time, rank, streak and comp roll history. Add @user to check someone else.
/leaderboard [top]
See the Hall of Dreamers. Sortable by points, rank, voice time or messages. Use top to change how many are shown.

Shop

/shop
Browse the dream shop. 10 items per page, sorted by price then alphabetically.
/buy <item>
Purchase an item from the shop. Points are deducted instantly.
/inventory [@user]
See the items you own. Add @user to check someone else's inventory.
/equip
Equip a purchased role from your inventory. Shows a dropdown of your owned roles.
/unequip
Unequip a role you currently have equipped. Only shows roles you are currently wearing.
/settitle <title>
Equip a purchased title to display on your /points profile. Leave empty to remove your title.
/setcustomtitle <text>
Use a Custom Title item to set your own unique title (max 32 characters). Consumes the item on use.
/rolepreview <item>
Preview a role item's colour before buying.

Comp Items

/useitem
Queue a comp item before the next /randomcomp you're in.
/cancelitem
Cancel your currently queued comp items.

Tracking

/registerriot <name#tag> <region>
Add a Riot account to server tracking. Automatically tracks both Valorant RR and TFT.
/unregisterriot <name#tag>
Remove a Riot account from server tracking.

Stats & Leaderboards

/valleaderboard
See the Valorant RR leaderboard for registered players.
/valduos [player1] [player2]
Duo leaderboard for tracked players, or look up a specific pair.
/valvs <player1> <player2>
Head-to-head stats comparison between two tracked Valorant players.
/valstats <name#tag> <region> [detail]
Show Valorant stats for any player. Detail options: clutch, utility, behaviour, agents, maps.
/footshot <name#tag>
Check a player's shot accuracy across their last 10 competitive games.
/scoreboard [match id / username]
Show the scoreboard for a match. Provide a match ID or a username to use their latest game, or reply to an RR update with r!sb.
/valclutches
Clutch leaderboard for all tracked players, based on cached matches.
/valtrend <name#tag>
Performance trend — compares recent games vs older cached games.

Comp Roller

/randomagent [role]
Get a random Valorant agent, optionally filtered by role.
/randomrole
Get a random Valorant role.
/randomcomp
Randomly assign 5 players to Valorant roles. Respects queued comp items — see Comp Items.

TFT

TFT tracking is enabled automatically for any account added with /registerriot, there's no separate registration command.

/tftleaderboard
Show the TFT LP leaderboard for all tracked accounts. Fetches live LP on demand.
/tftstats <name#tag> <region>
Show detailed TFT ranked stats for any player — wins, losses, winrate and rank.

Daily Shop

Lets you check your personal Valorant storefront from Discord. This is separate from the rank/stat tracking above, and requires linking your own Riot account via a secure DM flow — your password is only ever typed into Riot's own login page, never stored.

/linkriot
Link your Riot account (via DM) to check your daily shop.
/dailyshop [member]
See your (or a linked friend's) daily Valorant shop.
/unlinkriot
Remove your linked Riot account from this bot.

Anonymous Q&A

/answer

Guest Invites

/guestinvite
Generate a one-use guest invite — guest is moved to your VC when they join one, and removed if they leave it.
/drag @member
Drag a member into your current voice channel.

Points & Shop

/addpoints @user <amount>
Add or remove dream points from a member.
/additem
Add an item to the dream shop.
/removeitem
Remove an item from the dream shop.
/edititem
Edit an existing item in the dream shop.
/setshopchannel #channel
Set the channel for the permanent, self-updating shop interface.
/refreshshop
Force-rebuild and re-post the permanent shop embed.

Valorant & TFT

/setvalchannel #channel
Set the channel for Valorant RR and TFT tracking updates and the daily summary.
/settftchannel #channel
Set the channel for TFT LP tracking updates.
/valtrackerstatus
Check if the RR tracker poll is running and see the status of all registered accounts.
/valtrackertest <name#tag>
Test the Henrik API for a specific account and see the raw response.
/valforcepost <name#tag>
Force-post the most recent match for a tracked account and update streak.
/valcache
Show how many matches are cached for each tracked account.
/valbackfillstreak
Backfill win/loss streaks from match history for all tracked accounts.

Anonymous Q&A

/addquestion <text>
Add a question to the anonymous pool.
/removequestion
Remove a question from the anonymous pool.
/listquestions
List all questions in the pool.
/setanswerchannel #channel
Set the channel where anonymous answers are posted.
/setguessingrole @role
Set the role required to guess in the anonymous game.
/setguesstimeout <hours>
Set how many hours guessing stays open.
/setanonymouspoints <amount>
Set points awarded to the answerer after 3 wrong guesses.
/setguesspoints <amount>
Set points awarded for a correct guess.

Recap & Comp Winners

/setrecapchannel #channel
Set the channel where the weekly recap is posted every Monday at midnight UTC.
/sendrecap [week:YYYY-MM-DD]
Manually trigger the weekly recap. Optionally specify a week start date.
/setcompwinnerrole @role
Set the shared role given to all comp roll winners each week.

Guests & Moderation

/setinviterole @role
Set the role required to generate guest invites.
/setlingeringrole @role
Set the role that members must have to be dragged with /drag.
/mudaecleaner enabled:<bool> [delay:<duration>]
Enable or disable the Mudae roll cleaner. Set the delay before unclaimed rolls are deleted (e.g. 3h). Defaults to 3 hours.
