📖 Commands

everything Reverie can do

Everyone

/points
Check your dream points, voice time, rank, streak and comp roll history. Add @user to check someone else.
/leaderboard
See the Hall of Dreamers. Sortable by points, rank, voice time or messages. Use top to change how many are shown.
/shop
Browse the dream shop. 10 items per page, sorted by price then alphabetically.
/buy <item>
Purchase an item from the shop. Points are deducted instantly.
/inventory
See the items you own. Add @user to check someone else's inventory.
/equip
Equip a purchased role from your inventory. Shows a dropdown of your owned roles.
/unequip
Unequip a role you currently have equipped. Only shows roles you are currently wearing.
/settitle <title>
Equip a purchased title to display on your /points profile. Leave empty to remove your title.
/setcustomtitle <text>
Use a Custom Title item to set your own unique title (max 32 characters). Consumes the item on use. Buy another to change it.
/rolepreview <item>
Preview a role item's colour before buying. The embed sidebar shows the exact role colour.
/removerole
Use a Role Remover item to remove one of your purchased shop roles.
/answer
Answer today's anonymous question. A modal popup appears with a random question - answer once per day.
/dashboard
Posts the dashboard link in chat with a preview image.

Valorant

/randomagent
Get a random Valorant agent. Use the role option to filter by Duelist, Initiator, Controller or Sentinel.
/randomrole
Get a random Valorant role.
/randomcomp
Randomly assign 5 members to Valorant roles. Enable roll_agents to also roll a random agent for each role. Results are tracked for the weekly recap.
/registerriot <name#tag> <region>
Add a Riot account to server tracking. Automatically tracks both Valorant RR and TFT LP. Games are detected within 1 minute of finishing.
/unregisterriot <name#tag>
Remove a Riot account from server tracking.
/valleaderboard
See the current rank and RR for all registered players, sorted by ELO.
/valstats <name#tag> <region>
Show Valorant ranked stats for any player - rank, RR, win/loss record and peak rank.
/tftleaderboard
See the TFT LP leaderboard for all tracked accounts. Fetches live LP on demand.
/tftstats <name#tag> <region>
Show detailed TFT ranked stats for any player - wins, losses, winrate and rank.
/scoreboard
Show the scoreboard for a match. Provide a match ID or a username to use their latest game. Also works by replying to an RR update with r!sb.
/footshot <name#tag>
Check a player's headshot, bodyshot and legshot percentages across their last 20 competitive games.

Invite role only

/guestinvite
Generate a one-use guest invite that expires in 10 minutes. The invite link is sent to your DMs. When the guest joins the server and enters any voice channel they are moved to your VC automatically. They are kicked when they leave, or after 1 hour if they never join a VC.
/drag @member
Drag a member with the lingering role into your current voice channel. Both you and the target must be in a VC.

Admin only

/addpoints @user <amount>
Manually add or remove dream points from a member. Use a negative number to remove points.
/additem
Add an item to the dream shop. Choose from Role, Title, Custom Title or Role Remover. Can also be done from the dashboard.
/removeitem <name>
Remove an item from the shop by name. Can also be done from the dashboard.
/edititem <name>
Edit an existing shop item's name, cost, description or linked role. Can also be done from the dashboard.
/setinviterole @role
Set the role required to generate guest invites and use /drag.
/setlingeringrole @role
Set the role that members must have to be dragged with /drag.
/setanswerchannel #channel
Set the channel where anonymous answers are posted.
/setguessingrole @role
Set the role required to guess in the anonymous Q&A game.
/setguesstimeout <hours>
Set how many hours guessing stays open after an answer is posted.
/setanonymouspoints <amount>
Set the points awarded to the answerer for surviving 3 wrong guesses.
/setguesspoints <amount>
Set the points awarded for a correct guess.
/addquestion <text>
Add a question to the anonymous Q&A pool.
/removequestion <text>
Remove a question from the pool by its exact text.
/listquestions
List all questions currently in the anonymous Q&A pool.
/setrecapchannel #channel
Set the channel where the weekly recap is posted every Monday at midnight UTC.
/sendrecap
Manually trigger this week's recap.
/setvalchannel #channel
Set the channel for Valorant RR and TFT tracking updates and the daily summary.
/valtrackerstatus
Check if the RR tracker poll is running and see the status of all registered accounts.
/valtrackertest <name#tag>
Test the Henrik API for a specific account and see the raw response.
/settftchannel #channel
Set the channel for TFT LP tracking updates.
